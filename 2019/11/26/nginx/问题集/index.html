  1. unknown directive “stream” in /etc/nginx/nginx.conf

unknown directive “stream” in /etc/nginx/nginx.conf

环境：centos7，nginx是用yum install的

解决：在/etc/nginx/nginx.conf的第一行插入：
load_module /usr/lib64/nginx/modules/ngx_stream_module.so;

原文作者: Buggoing

原文链接: https://buggoing.github.io/2019/11/26/nginx/问题集/
